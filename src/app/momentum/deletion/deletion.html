Momentum Account Deletion Request

This page provides instructions for users who wish to request the deletion of their Momentum account and associated data.

To request account deletion:

Please send an email to joshuamwaurangugi@gmail.com.

In the subject line, please write:

Account Deletion Request - [Your Momentum Email Address]

In the body of the email, please state your explicit request for account deletion. For verification purposes, you may be asked to confirm additional details related to your account.

What data will be deleted?

Upon successful verification and processing of your request:

  • Your user account (email, unique user ID) within Firebase Authentication will be deleted.
  • All photos and videos you have uploaded to Firebase Storage will be permanently removed.
  • All associated metadata stored in Cloud Firestore (including post details, timestamps, etc.) linked to your user ID will be permanently removed.

What data might be kept?

Please note that we may retain certain data for a limited period where required by law, for tax purposes, or to ensure the security and integrity of our services (e.g., standard server logs or audit trails that do not contain personally identifiable information specific to your content). Any such retention will comply with applicable data protection laws.

Processing Time:

We will endeavor to process your account deletion request within 90 days of receiving and verifying your request. You will receive an email confirmation once your account and data have been deleted.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at joshuamwaurangugi@gmail.com.
